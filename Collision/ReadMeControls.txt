Controls:

Press 'space bar' to trigger debug mode

Press 'r' to trigger a random ball drop

Press 't' to trigger a ball drop on the top face

Press 'u' to move target triangle left to right

Press 'i' to move target triangle right to left

Press 'd' to drop a ball on a target

Press 'e' to clear all balls on screen

Press 's' to switch from predefined double drop or target/inverted target double drop

Press 'f' to double drop

Press 'g' to remove the bottom 12 triangles

Press 'h' to reenable all the triangles

Press 'up arrow key' to increment available balls for all drop

Press 'down arrow key' to decrement available balls for all drop

Press 'b' to drop all available balls

press 'n' to cycle targets in a circle

*unfornately ran out of time to go spiral backwards

press 'm' to switch through available heightmaps
